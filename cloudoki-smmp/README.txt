== Description ==

*The Plugin is currently in active development - use at own risk*

**Cloudoki SMMP** is a Social Media Management Plugin for Wordpress.
SMMP has an integral WP implementation, no outside smms services are required. 
SMMP is developed for business critical environments, including failed publication logs and notice mails for outdated service connections.

Cloudoki SMMP supports following Social Media Platforms:

### Facebook
Connect your Facebook account with your blog to publish both on your timeline as your connected Facebook Pages.

### Twitter
Publish on your Twitter stream

### Phase 2
Publishing on Instagram, Pinterest, Google+ and LinkedIn are planned for development.

### Feature List
View the Cloudoki SMMP feature list on [https://github.com/Cloudoki/wordpress-smmp](https://github.com/Cloudoki/wordpress-smmp) - README.md

== Installation ==

1. Upload the SMMP folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Connect your preferred Social Media accounts
1. Add your publication list to the dashboard
1. Start publishing in your post fields
